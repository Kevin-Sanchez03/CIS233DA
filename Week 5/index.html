Sedona is renowned for its stunning red sandstone formations that create a unique and picturesque landscape. These red rocks have been featured in numerous films and are a major draw for outdoor enthusiasts, photographers, and nature lovers.

Sedona is designated as an International Dark Sky Community, meaning it has taken measures to preserve the natural darkness of the night sky. This makes it an excellent destination for stargazing and astronomy enthusiasts.

Sedona offers a wide range of outdoor activities, including hiking, mountain biking, jeep tours, and hot air balloon rides. With over 100 hiking trails, including the famous Cathedral Rock and Devil's Bridge, there's something for every adventurer.
